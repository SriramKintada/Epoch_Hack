four weekends that change everything

Most accelerators give you 3 months and a badge. We give you 4 weekends and a product people actually pay for.

Weekend 1

The Foundation

You walk in with an idea (or nothing at all). You walk out with clarity.

We kill bad ideas fast. We validate good ones faster. Co-founder matching happens here—not on LinkedIn, but in the room where it matters. Market validation isn't a buzzword; it's 48 hours of brutal honesty about whether people will actually pay for what you're building.

By Sunday night, you know exactly what you're building and who you're building it with.

Weekend 2

The Build

This is where talk becomes code. Where slides become software.

You get access to infrastructure most early-stage founders dream about: unlimited AI credits, cloud compute, and developers who've shipped real products. Mid-week, successful founders join online to share what actually worked—not theory, but tactics.

By the end, you have something you can show. Something that works. Something real.

Weekend 3

The Market

Your product meets reality. Reality wins. Then you iterate.

Real users. Real feedback. Real money conversations. This is where you learn if people will pay, how much they'll pay, and what needs to change before they do. Distribution isn't something you think about later—it's built into the product from day one.

If you're not uncomfortable this weekend, you're not pushing hard enough.

Weekend 4

The Launch

Everything converges here. Product. Traction. Story. Capital.

You're not just launching a product—you're opening a channel to customers, investors, and partners who can 10x what you've built. Strategic introductions happen in this room. Fundraising conversations start here. Media coverage amplifies everything.

By the end of month one, you're not a side project anymore. You're a company.

What Happens After

Epoch alumni don't disappear. They build together, invest in each other, and open doors most founders spend years trying to unlock. You get permanent access to the community, the workspace, the network, and the people who actually understand what you're going through.

This isn't a program. It's a launchpad. And once you're in, you're in for life.

Why only 20?

Because density matters more than scale. We've seen what happens when you put 20 exceptional builders in one room for a month—ideas collide, co-founders meet, and products get built at a velocity that shocks even us.

Every cohort after this will point back to Cohort 0 as "the one that started it." The founding teams who were there when Epoch was just an idea itself.

You either get this opportunity, or you spend the next year watching from the sidelines.

faq

who should apply?
High-agency builders of any age or background. If you're serious about shipping a product in one month and can commit to 4 intense weekends, you're a fit. We care about execution ability, not credentials.
do I need a team to apply?
No. Stage Zero includes co-founder matching. Come solo or with a team. We'll help you find the right collaborators if needed.
what if I don't have an idea?
Perfect. We maintain a bank of validated ideas including YC-approved models and proven US/foreign concepts that can be adapted for India. Week 1 focuses on idea selection and validation.
is this equity-free?
Yes. Epoch takes no equity. We're building a high-signal pipeline of fundable companies, not extracting value from founders. Our success is your success.
what's the selection process?
Heavy multi-platform social media promotion creates a large applicant pool. We run a rigorous filtration process focusing on agency, technical ability, and commitment. Small, highly curated cohort.
where is this happening?
Pune, India. Large dedicated office space with full weekend access. Mid-week sessions are online to accommodate builders who can't relocate.
